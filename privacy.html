🤿 COSMIQ+ Manager — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

COSMIQ+ Manager is a free, open-source web app that helps users download and manage dive logs from their Deepblu COSMIQ+ dive computer. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

COSMIQ+ Manager collects the following data:

  • Dive log data — depth profiles, timestamps, temperature readings downloaded from your dive computer via Bluetooth
  • User-entered data — dive site names, conditions, gear details, notes and ratings you add manually
  • Photos — images you choose to upload to your dive logs

How We Store Your Data

All your data is stored exclusively in two places that you control:

  • Your browser's local storage — on the device you use the app on
  • Your own Google Drive — in a folder called "COSMIQ Manager", only if you choose to connect Google Drive

We do not operate any servers. We do not store your data anywhere else. The developer has no access to your dive logs, photos, or personal information.

Google Drive Access

If you choose to connect Google Drive, COSMIQ+ Manager uses the drive.file scope, which means:

  • The app can only read and write files that it created itself
  • The app cannot access any other files in your Google Drive
  • The app cannot access your Gmail, Calendar, or any other Google service
  • You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your dive logs and photos are never transmitted to our servers because we have none.

Bluetooth Access

The app uses your device's Bluetooth to communicate directly with your COSMIQ+ dive computer. No Bluetooth data is transmitted over the internet.

Cookies and Tracking

COSMIQ+ Manager does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

COSMIQ+ Manager is an unofficial, open-source project and is not affiliated with Deepblu.
